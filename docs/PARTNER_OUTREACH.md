I run Azur Menton, a small family-run short-stay apartment website for guests staying in central Menton.

We are preparing a simple guest welcome selection with local samples, vouchers or small perks from businesses in Menton and nearby. If this could be useful for you, we can start with around 10-15 units per month and place them directly in the apartments for arriving guests.

If you are interested, please send a short description of what you would like to offer and any practical conditions.

Kind regards,
Azur Menton

## Internal Policy Checklist

Acceptable sample types:
- Sealed local food items with clear labels and dates.
- Small non-food welcome items that are clean, useful and easy to store.
- Printed vouchers, tasting invitations or discount cards.
- Small guest perks that are simple to explain and redeem.

Packaging and labelling:
- Items should be sealed or individually packaged.
- Labels should identify the product, partner name and any key ingredients.
- Expiry or best-before dates must be visible when relevant.
- Avoid messy, leaking, fragile, bulky or strongly scented items.

Monthly volume:
- Start with approximately 10-15 units per month.
- Review guest fit and logistics before accepting larger quantities.

Allergy and safety:
- Food, cosmetics or wellness items should include ingredient/allergen information.
- Do not accept expired, unlabelled or unclear products.
- Refuse anything that feels unsafe, impractical or unsuitable for short-stay guests.

Alcohol and age-restricted products:
- Treat alcohol and age-restricted items with extra care.
- Prefer vouchers or partner-location tastings rather than physical bottles in apartments.
- Do not place age-restricted items where guest age cannot be reliably controlled.

Website and commercial visibility:
- Free sampling does not guarantee website placement, guide inclusion or public promotion.
- Any paid or sponsored visibility must be handled separately and clearly disclosed.
- Sponsored links or placements should be marked appropriately before publication.
